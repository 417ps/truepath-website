About TruePath Data Centers

Convergence of Expertise and Opportunity

Company Overview

TruePath Data Centers represents the convergence of decades of mission-critical data center expertise with innovative real estate investment strategies. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Albuquerque, New Mexico, we are positioned to capitalize on the unprecedented demand for data center infrastructure in the Southwest United States.

Our company operates as a vertically integrated real estate investment and development firm specializing exclusively in data center facilities. Unlike traditional data center builders who construct for others, TruePath Data Centers maintains complete control from land acquisition and power purchase agreements through construction, operation, and eventual monetization of these critical infrastructure assets.

How We Were Formed

Early Years
Michael Sanchez first entered a data center at age 16 while working for his father's construction company at Los Alamos National Laboratory. This early exposure to mission-critical computing infrastructure would define a career trajectory spanning over two decades.
Building Expertise
Throughout college and beyond, continued working in supercomputing facilities, gaining intimate knowledge of the challenges inherent in supporting the world's fastest computers. After earning dual bachelor's degrees in Business Marketing and Hotel Restaurant Management, gained corporate leadership experience with Four Seasons and Jason's Deli.
Scaling Success
Returning to Cross Connection, transformed a business that had plateaued at $4 million annual revenue. Through systematic improvements in project management, team development, and operational efficiency, the company grew to $30 million in annual revenue within a decade.
Real Estate Evolution
Transitioned into real estate investment, mastering single-family fix-and-flips before advancing to multifamily investments. This pivot provided crucial expertise in real estate acquisition and development, private capital raising, legal structures and tax optimization, and investment analysis and portfolio management.
2024: The Convergence
As multifamily investment opportunities became increasingly competitive, recognized an unprecedented opportunity to combine data center expertise with real estate investment acumen. The explosive growth in AI and data center demand, coupled with unique background and relationships, created the perfect conditions for TruePath Data Centers.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build the best, most efficient, and most investable data centers in the Southwest United States by leveraging technical expertise, creativity, and innovative investment strategies to deliver superior returns while advancing the nation's digital infrastructure.

Our vision for the future is that we really want to show what most people haven't been able to see. There's a lot of cutting-edge technology that has been utilized out there and different iterations that don't keep up with the market as fast as they need to, with the data center space moving so quickly and moving so profoundly with so much money going into it.

We want to use our experience in the cutting edge technologies that we've seen operated and have data on and be one of the leaders in implementing these as quickly and correctly as possible so that we can offer to our tenants who really want to win the AI race.
