SEC Filings

Regulatory compliance and transparency for our investors

Pre-Launch Phase: SEC filings will be available upon commencement of capital raising activities

SEC Filing Timeline

TruePath Data Centers is committed to full regulatory compliance and transparency. As we prepare for our capital raising activities, we will file all required documents with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Phase 1: Pre-Filing
Currently preparing all necessary documentation and conducting due diligence reviews with our legal and accounting teams.
Phase 2: Initial Filing
Form D will be filed within 15 days of the first sale of securities, along with all required state blue sky filings.
Phase 3: Ongoing Compliance
Regular updates and amendments will be filed as required, with all documents made available to investors.

Expected SEC Filings

Once our capital raising activities commence, the following documents will be filed and made available:

Form D - Notice of Exempt Offering

  • Filing deadline: Within 15 days of first sale
  • Contains basic information about the offering and company
  • Includes details about exemption claimed and amount of securities offered

Private Placement Memorandum (PPM)

  • Comprehensive disclosure document for investors
  • Detailed business plan and financial projections
  • Risk factors and investment terms
  • Management team backgrounds and experience

Operating Agreements

  • Limited Liability Company Operating Agreement
  • Investment management agreements
  • Subscription agreements and investor representations

Regulatory Framework

Our offering will be structured under the following regulatory framework:

Rule 506(c) - General Solicitation Permitted

This exemption allows us to publicly advertise our investment opportunity while maintaining compliance with federal securities laws. All investors must be verified as accredited investors.

Accredited Investor Verification

We work with qualified third parties to verify accredited investor status in accordance with SEC requirements, ensuring full compliance with federal regulations.

State Securities Laws

In addition to federal filings, we comply with all applicable state securities laws and regulations in jurisdictions where we offer securities.

Important Disclosure

Document Access

Once filed, all SEC documents will be available through:

  • Quarterly investor updates and reports
  • Annual compliance reporting
