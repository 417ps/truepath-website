Executive Summary

A compelling opportunity to invest in transformative AI infrastructure

TruePath Data Centers presents a compelling opportunity to invest in a transformative 200MW data center development on Albuquerque's west mesa. This strategically positioned campus combines proven operational expertise with cutting-edge technology to capture the explosive growth in AI and high-performance computing infrastructure demand. The project leverages a unique confluence of abundant power resources, favorable tax structures, and institutional-quality partnerships to deliver exceptional risk-adjusted returns.

The Albuquerque market represents an emerging nexus for data center development, with access to 600MW of potential power capacity through future agreements with leading utilities and energy providers. Our team is pursuing critical infrastructure commitments from Tier 4 data center operators and has established strategic alliances with industry leaders including Submer Technologies for next-generation immersion cooling systems. This foundation positions TruePath to deliver a state-of-the-art facility meeting the exacting requirements of hyperscale and AI workload customers.
