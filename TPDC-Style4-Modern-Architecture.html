TruePath is a full-fledged data center development and real estate investment company focused on the Southwest region. Our expertise spans New Mexico, Arizona, Colorado, Texas, and Utah, where we identify and develop emerging markets with essential power and fiber infrastructure.

Leveraging our DOE and supercomputer backgrounds, we build cutting-edge, energy-efficient data centers with smaller footprints. Our technological advancements and green building practices position us at the forefront of sustainable data center development throughout the Southwest.
