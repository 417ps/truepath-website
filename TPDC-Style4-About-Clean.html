Building the Future of Data Centers

Where mission-critical expertise meets commercial innovation

25 Years of Mission-Critical Operations

Our team spent decades working in the supercomputing facilities at Los Alamos and Sandia National Laboratories. These environments demanded absolute reliability. Equipment failures could derail multi-year research projects. Temperature variations could corrupt irreplaceable data. Power interruptions meant millions in lost computational time.

Through hands-on experience building, operating, and maintaining these critical systems, we developed expertise that goes beyond industry standards. This background in environments where failure wasn't an option shapes everything we build today.

Expertise Born from Unique Challenges

Resource Optimization

National laboratory facilities required maximum efficiency per square foot. Limited power allocations drove innovative cooling approaches. Budget scrutiny created lasting cost discipline.

Reliability Systems

Developed predictive maintenance protocols. Created testing procedures that never interrupted operations. Built monitoring systems tracking metrics beyond industry standards.

Operational Precision

Maintained environments where 0.1°C variations mattered. Worked with petabyte-scale storage when capacity was limited. Coordinated maintenance windows months in advance.

Bringing Laboratory Standards to Industry

The disciplines developed in national laboratories translate directly to commercial needs. The same cooling innovations that managed supercomputer heat loads now enable 10x density improvements. The predictive maintenance protocols ensure consistent uptime. The efficiency mindset creates significant operational cost savings.

TruePath applies these proven methodologies to build, own, and operate next-generation data centers throughout the Southwest. We maintain complete control from site selection through daily operations, ensuring the same exacting standards that kept critical research running without interruption.

The Southwest Advantage

Our strategic focus on the Southwest leverages natural advantages that amplify our operational excellence. The region offers ideal climate conditions for our advanced cooling systems, competitive energy costs, and growing connectivity to major markets. Combined with favorable tax structures and available land, the Southwest provides the perfect environment for deploying our proven approach at scale.
