Our Story

From national laboratories to the next generation of data infrastructure

01

The Genesis

TruePath Data Centers emerged from a unique confluence of expertise and opportunity. Our founder, Michael Sanchez, first entered a data center at age 16 while working for his father's construction company at Los Alamos National Laboratory.

This early exposure to mission-critical computing infrastructure would define a career trajectory spanning over two decades, building and operating data centers for the world's fastest supercomputers.

Today, we leverage this unparalleled background to deliver next-generation data center solutions to meet the explosive demands of AI and digital transformation.

Our Journey

2000s

Building Expertise

Gained intimate knowledge of data center operations at Los Alamos and Sandia National Laboratories, supporting systems critical to national security.

2010s

Scaling Success

Transformed Cross Connection from $4M to $30M annual revenue through systematic improvements in project management and operational efficiency.

2020s

Real Estate Evolution

Mastered real estate investment, private capital raising, and portfolio management, setting the stage for TruePath's unique approach.

2024

TruePath Founded

Combining decades of data center expertise with innovative real estate strategies to revolutionize Southwest data infrastructure.
