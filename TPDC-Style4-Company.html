About TruePath

Redefining Data Center Excellence

Mission-Critical Expertise Meets Innovation

Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Albuquerque, New Mexico, TruePath Data Centers represents the convergence of decades of mission-critical data center expertise with innovative real estate investment strategies.

Our team brings together over 100 years of combined hands-on experience building, retrofitting, designing, operating, and maintaining data centers for supercomputing facilities at Los Alamos and Sandia National Laboratories.

Unlike traditional data center builders who construct for others, TruePath maintains complete control from land acquisition and power purchase agreements through construction, operation, and eventual monetization of these critical infrastructure assets.
