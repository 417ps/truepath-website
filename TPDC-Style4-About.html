Our Story

Mission-Critical Expertise Meets Innovation

Building the Future of Digital Infrastructure

Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Albuquerque, New Mexico, TruePath Data Centers represents the convergence of decades of mission-critical data center expertise with innovative real estate investment strategies.

Our company operates as a vertically integrated real estate investment and development firm specializing exclusively in data center facilities. Unlike traditional data center builders who construct for others, TruePath maintains complete control from land acquisition and power purchase agreements through construction, operation, and eventual monetization of these critical infrastructure assets.

We are building a company with diversified revenue streams within the data center industry, positioning ourselves to capitalize on multiple opportunities across the Southwest region.
