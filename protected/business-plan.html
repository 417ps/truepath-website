Investment Opportunity

The Opportunity

The explosive growth of artificial intelligence and cloud computing has created an unprecedented demand for sophisticated data center infrastructure. Current industry capacity utilization exceeds 95% in primary markets, with new AI workloads requiring 10-50x the power density of traditional computing. This structural supply-demand imbalance presents a compelling opportunity for strategic data center development in emerging markets with abundant power resources.

TruePath Data Centers has identified Albuquerque as an optimal location for next-generation data center development. The region offers a unique combination of available power capacity, favorable economics, and strategic positioning between major West Coast and Central US markets. Our comprehensive market analysis indicates immediate demand for 200MW of capacity, with expansion potential to 600MW as hyperscale customers seek alternatives to constrained primary markets.

Value Creation Strategy

Our investment thesis centers on three core value drivers that differentiate this opportunity from traditional data center developments:

Technology Leadership

Partnership with Submer Technologies enables 350kW per rack density through advanced immersion cooling, delivering 3x the capacity of air-cooled facilities in the same footprint. This technology advantage translates directly to superior unit economics and customer value proposition.

Strategic Tax Structuring

Leveraging Qualified Opportunity Zone designation provides investors with immediate tax deferral, 15% basis step-up after seven years, and complete capital gains elimination after ten years. Combined with New Mexico's competitive tax environment, this structure enhances after-tax returns by approximately 600 basis points.

Operational Excellence

Our team's proven track record includes developing and operating over $20 billion in mission-critical infrastructure. This experience enables accelerated development timelines, optimized construction costs, and superior operational performance that directly impacts investment returns.

Market Dynamics

The data center market is experiencing a fundamental shift driven by AI workload requirements. Traditional facilities designed for 5-15kW per rack cannot economically serve AI clusters requiring 50-350kW densities. This technology transition creates a generational opportunity for purpose-built facilities optimized for high-density computing.

Key market indicators supporting our investment thesis include:

  • Supply Constraints: Less than 2% vacancy in Tier 1 markets with 18-24 month delivery times for new capacity
  • Demand Acceleration: AI infrastructure spending projected to reach $400 billion annually by 2027
  • Power Scarcity: Major markets facing power moratoriums, driving customers to emerging locations
  • Economic Shifts: Enterprises prioritizing total cost of ownership over traditional market preferences
