The Southwest Advantage

Strategic Geographic Positioning

While traditional data center markets face constraints of land availability, power costs, and regulatory complexity, the Southwest presents a compelling alternative for next-generation data center development.

Our strategic focus on the Southwest United States leverages unique regional advantages that enable us to deliver superior returns while meeting the explosive demand for data center capacity.

  • ✓ Power costs 30-40% below coastal markets
  • ✓ Land costs 50-70% below traditional hubs
  • ✓ Non-humid climate ideal for cooling efficiency
  • ✓ Expanding fiber connectivity to major markets
  • ✓ Tax incentives and opportunity zone benefits
